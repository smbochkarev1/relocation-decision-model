Fit, access and safety point at different countries

The trade-off

Georgia tops the score but scores worst on geopolitics. Portugal & Spain are safe and pleasant — but naturalization just moved 5 → 10 years. UAE is safe with an easy visa — yet has no path to a passport at all.

Why keep them separate

A single ranking hides this tension. Scoring fit, legalization access and safety as three lenses is what turns a wish-list into a decision — and lets you re-weight for your own risk tolerance.
